About

Meira Davidson

Meira Davidson writes about the basics — finding the backbone of a subject, the few ideas everything else is built on, and explaining them from the ground up.

The hope is simple: that a reader feels the quiet pleasure of figuring something out, and sees how little it takes to understand what matters.
